Banana Pancakes

Ingredients are ->
  • 1/2 cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Blended with Real Bananas
  • 1 ripe banana mashed (plus more for serving)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon real vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder not baking soda

"Banana Pancakes are a simple, tasty, healthy recipe you can make for breakfast every morning! These pancakes are made with almondmilk, bananas, oats, and lots of other good stuff. These HEALTHY Banana Pancakes simply feel too good to be true. All the flavor of delicious pancakes, but with amazing ingredient alternatives to make them healthier. So you can have pancakes for breakfast while still feeling good the rest of the day! "
  • Instructions :
    1. Place Almond Breeze Almondmilk Blended with Real Bananas, banana, egg and egg whites, honey, oats, cinnamon, salt, and vanilla in a high powered blender.
    2. Blend until completely smooth.
    3. Heat large griddle or cast iron skillet over medium heat. Spray with nonstick spray.
    4. Use 1/3 cup measuring cup to pour batter onto griddle in a circle.
    5. Cook for 2-3 minutes for the first side, watching for the batter to begin to bubble before flipping.
    6. Turn and cook for 1-2 more minutes, or until pancake is browned on both sides to your liking.
    7. Put the toppings and extras. And enjoy!

Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients are ->
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cup all-purpose flour plus 2 teaspoons for blueberries
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder1
  • 1 ½ cup fresh blueberries

"Making muffins from scratch has never been easier! This easy Blueberry Muffins Recipe serves up soft muffins with a sugary, crumble top and of course LOTS of blueberries! A berry delicious snack, breakfast or dessert!"
  • Instructions :
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
    2. Mix together oil and sugar until fluffy.
    3. Whisk in sour cream, buttermilk, egg, and vanilla until just combined.
    4. Add in 1 ½ cups flour, salt, and baking powder and stir. Do not overmix.
    5. Toss fresh blueberries with 2 teaspoons flour until all the blueberries are coated.
    6. Add blueberries into mixture and gently fold until incorporated, making sure not to break any of the berries.
    7. Spoon blueberry mixture into a lined or well-greased muffin tin until each tin is 2/3 full.
    8. Prepare brown sugar topping by stirring together brown sugar, flour, and melted butter.
    9. Sprinkle each muffin with a generous coating of brown sugar topping.
    10. Bake in oven for 20 to 25 minutes or until muffins spring back when touched or toothpick comes out clean.
