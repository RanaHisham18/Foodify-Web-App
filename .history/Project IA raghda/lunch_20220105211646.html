Rigatoni with Mushroom Sauce

The ingredients are:

  • Rigatoni pasta
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Chicken stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Fresh herbs (we’ve used chives and parsley)
  • Parmesan cheese

The Instructions are:
  1. Heat the oil and butter in a skillet. We love the taste of both butter and a good olive oil for sautéing the mushrooms.
  2. Add the mushrooms and garlic.
  3. Add the chicken stock and reduce it down
  4. Next, add the cream and parmesan, along with the fresh herbs.
  5. Season your sauce with salt and pepper.
  6. Lastly, add your cooked rigatoni pasta
