Creamy chicken tortilla soup

Ingredients are ->
  • Chicken
  • Chicken stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Corn
  • Garlic
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Jalapenos
  • Black beans
  • Tortilla

" This easy chicken tortilla soup recipe has all of my favorite things. I’m a big fan of Mexican and Tex-Mex dishes, so of course this soup is a favorite. It’s loaded with everything I’d put in tacos or burritos, but in soup form."

Make this delicious soup recipe with ease! Making Chicken Tortilla Soup is a simple process, easy enough to make any time. You can even make it ahead if time during weekend meal prep, and refrigerate it for later in the week.

  1. Crush tortilla chips and chop up veggies according to the ingredient list, & set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in dutch oven over medium heat.
  3. Add onions to the pot and cook 3 minutes, then add jalapenos and cook for 1 more minute, then add garlic and cook for 30 more seconds.
  4. Pour in chicken broth, corn, beans, tomatoes, crushed tortilla chips, & spices and reduce heat.
  5. Place whole chicken breasts into the pot and let the soup simmer for 20 minutes (chicken will cook through during this time).
  6. Remove the chicken breasts from the pot and shred the meat, then return shredded chicken to the pot.
  7. Stir in the lime juice and heavy cream, and cook until fully heated through.

Baked Spaghetti Casserole

Ingredients are ->
  • Dry spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Corn
  • Garlic
  • tomato paste
  • Basil
  • shredded mozzarella
  • Unsalted butter

This Baked Spaghetti Recipe is a lifesaver when it comes to quickly preparing a homemade dinner. Sure, it doesn’t get much simpler than classic spaghetti (always a delicious choice!), but add extra cheese and bake it until toasty perfection and you’ve got a weeknight dinner winner. This is such a foolproof meal to make for your family after work, for Sunday night dinners, or even for gameday. It’s ready in less than an hour, no fancy ingredients required. This easy recipe is a favorite around here – cheesy pasta is always a good idea!
  1. Cook the spaghetti to al dente.
  2. Make the marinara meat sauce.
  3. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and stir to fully coat the spaghetti.
  4. Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle with cheese, and bake.
