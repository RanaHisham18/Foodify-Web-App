Banana Pancakes

Ingredients are ->
  • 1/2 cup Almond Breeze Almondmilk Blended with Real Bananas
  • 1 ripe banana mashed (plus more for serving)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon real vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder not baking soda

"Banana Pancakes are a simple, tasty, healthy recipe you can make for breakfast every morning! These pancakes are made with almondmilk, bananas, oats, and lots of other good stuff. These HEALTHY Banana Pancakes simply feel too good to be true. All the flavor of delicious pancakes, but with amazing ingredient alternatives to make them healthier. So you can have pancakes for breakfast while still feeling good the rest of the day! "
  • Instructions :
    1. Place Almond Breeze Almondmilk Blended with Real Bananas, banana, egg and egg whites, honey, oats, cinnamon, salt, and vanilla in a high powered blender.
    2. Blend until completely smooth.
    3. Heat large griddle or cast iron skillet over medium heat. Spray with nonstick spray.
    4. Use 1/3 cup measuring cup to pour batter onto griddle in a circle.
    5. Cook for 2-3 minutes for the first side, watching for the batter to begin to bubble before flipping.
    6. Turn and cook for 1-2 more minutes, or until pancake is browned on both sides to your liking.
    7. Put the toppings and extras. And enjoy!
